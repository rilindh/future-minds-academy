Project 1

In this project, I've created a webpage showcasing three captivating tourist destinations in Albania. Each destination is presented with captivating images and engaging descriptions to inspire travelers to explore the wonders of Albania. Project 2

In this project, I've crafted a page dedicated to the iconic footballer Lionel Messi. The landing page presents a concise write-up about Messi, complemented by an audio segment and a video montage of some of his most stunning goals. Users can seamlessly navigate the site to delve into Messi's life story, the clubs he has played for, and some of his standout statistics. This project honors one of the football world's living legends, offering a comprehensive overview of his life, career, and extraordinary accomplishments.
