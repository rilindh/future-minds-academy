Most Beautiful Places to Visit in Albania

  • Blue Eye (Sarandë)

    The Blue Eye is a natural spring with a mesmerizing turquoise color that blends perfectly with the dark blue coming from its depth. It's one of the most beautiful places in Albania, and it has been described as magical and hypnotic. When seen from above, the spring resembles the human eye, so it was called Syri I Kaltër ,which means The blue eye in Albanian.

  • Butrint National Park

    Also falling into the category of the most beautiful places in Albania is Butrint National Park. Butrint was once among the most beautiful cities in the Roman Empire, and to this day, the ruins of the city attract tourists from all over the world. If you're a history buff, it's one of the best places to visit in Albania. Butrint is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with the ancient ruins being very valuable for the culture and legacy of the country. There are buses you can take, or you can drive your own car there. The roads are good, so you won't have any difficulties.
