Lionel Messi, often considered the greatest soccer player of all time, hails from Argentina and has had an illustrious career with two major clubs, FC Barcelona and Paris Saint-Germain (PSG). 
      Messi began his professional journey with Barcelona, where he spent over two decades, becoming the club's all-time leading scorer. 
      During his time with Barcelona, he secured numerous titles and won six Ballon d'Or awards. 
      In 2021, he moved to PSG, continuing his legacy by helping the team secure victory in France's top league and adding another Ballon d'Or to his impressive collection.
      With his incredible skills, Messi has redefined the game and earned a reputation as one of the greatest players in history.
      Now Lionel Messi plays for Inter Miami FC.
